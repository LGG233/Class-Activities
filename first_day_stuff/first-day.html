How to make a delicious ham and cheese wrap

  1. Get ingredients: wraps, ham, and cheese
  2. Put two slices of ham and three slices of cheese on wrap
  3. Put wrap into broiler oven
  4. Wait long enough for cheese to melt
  5. Roll up wrap with yummy melted cheese and hot ham, then EAT
